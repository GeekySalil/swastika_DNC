About The Firm

“We don't just design buildings — we craft spaces that hold meaning, light, and silence.”

Swastika Design & Constructions is a full-service architecture and construction practice based in Varanasi.

Founded by Ankur Pratap Singh, the firm is built on a single conviction — that thoughtful design is not a privilege of the few, but a standard every client deserves.

From the first pencil line to the final handover, every project passes through one disciplined team — architecture, visualization, interiors, and turnkey construction.
